About CoalPro

From humble beginnings in the coal fields of Mpumalanga to becoming a diversified multi-division powerhouse, CoalPro's journey spans over 25 years of innovation, growth, and strategic expansion across South Africa's most vital industries.

1999

The Foundation

CoalPro began as a specialized coal supplier in Witbank, Mpumalanga, serving local mills, distilleries, refineries, nurseries, and farms. Our core business was built on a simple promise: deliver high-quality coal with exceptional service. We established our own coal processing facility on-site, ensuring complete control over quality and supply chain efficiency.

2005

Energy Division Expansion

Recognizing the growing demand for diverse energy solutions, we expanded beyond coal to include premium diesel supply. Our Energy Division was formalized, offering A-Grade washed coal and 100% clean diesel to industrial clients across South Africa. This expansion allowed us to become a comprehensive energy solutions provider, working closely with clients to optimize efficiency and ensure timely delivery.

2012

Property Division Launch

Success in energy led to strategic diversification. We established our Property Division, initially focusing on acquiring and developing properties near our energy operations. This evolved into a full-service property company encompassing real estate listings, capital investment services, turn-key project solutions, and residential development. Our expertise in managing complex supply chains translated perfectly to property development and management.

2018

Agriculture Division Birth

Leveraging our deep relationships with farms and agricultural clients from our coal supply days, we identified an opportunity in the poultry sector. Our Agriculture Division was born with the establishment of CMD Broiler Site 1, featuring 10 broiler houses with a capacity of 300,000 chicks per cycle. This marked our entry into food production, applying the same principles of quality, efficiency, and reliability that built our energy business.

2023

Modern Expansion

Our commitment to growth culminated in the launch of CMD Broiler Site 2, adding 8 state-of-the-art broiler houses with 240,000 chickens per cycle capacity. This expansion represents our vision for the future: integrated operations across energy, property, and agriculture that support South Africa's economic development while maintaining our founding principles of excellence and reliability.

Our Philosophy

What started as a coal supply business has evolved into something much greater. We discovered that the principles that made us successful in energy – understanding our clients' needs, ensuring quality at every step, and building lasting relationships – apply equally to property development and agriculture. Today, CoalPro represents the convergence of three essential sectors that power South Africa's economy.

Our three divisions don't just coexist; they synergize. Our property expertise helps us develop optimal facilities for our agricultural operations. Our energy knowledge ensures efficient power solutions for our properties. Our agricultural understanding helps us serve farming clients better across all divisions. This integrated approach sets us apart in each sector we serve.

CoalPro Today

From our origins in the coal fields of Witbank to our modern multi-division operations, CoalPro has grown into a trusted partner across South Africa's most vital industries. We continue to process coal at our Mpumalanga facility, supply premium diesel to industrial clients, develop and manage properties, and operate state-of-the-art poultry facilities producing over 540,000 chickens per cycle.

Our success is measured not just in growth, but in the lasting relationships we've built with clients who have trusted us for decades. As we look to the future, we remain committed to the values that built our company: quality, reliability, innovation, and an unwavering focus on our clients' success.
